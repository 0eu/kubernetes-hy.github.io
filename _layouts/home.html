This course is an introductory course to Kubernetes with K3s and GKE. In this course you will develop software with microservice architecture that utilizes Course will also look into what different parts web services consist of such as reverse proxies, databases etc. Docker can not be installed on faculty computers so students will need to provide their own computers to follow the examples outlined in this course material and to complete the exercises.
